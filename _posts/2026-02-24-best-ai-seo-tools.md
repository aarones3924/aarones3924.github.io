## Content Optimization

### Surfer SEO - Best Content Optimizer

Surfer SEO analyzes top-ranking pages and tells you exactly what your content needs to rank. It's the closest thing to a Google ranking cheat sheet.

**How it works:**
1. Enter your target keyword
2. Surfer analyzes the top 10-20 ranking pages
3. Get a content score and optimization guidelines
4. Write or edit your content following the suggestions
5. Watch your content score improve in real-time

**What it tells you:**
- Optimal word count
- Keywords to include (and how many times)
- Headings structure
- Images needed
- NLP terms to include
- Internal linking suggestions

**AI features:**
- **AI Writer** — generates SEO-optimized articles
- **Auto-optimize** — AI rewrites sections to improve score
- **Outline builder** — AI creates article structure
- **SERP Analyzer** — understand why pages rank

**Price:** $89/month Essential / $179/month Scale

### ChatGPT for SEO

ChatGPT is surprisingly effective for SEO tasks when you know the right prompts.

**Keyword research:**
```
"Generate 30 long-tail keyword ideas for the topic 'AI tools 
for small business.' Group them by search intent: 
informational, commercial, transactional."
```

**Content outlines:**
```
"Analyze the top-ranking articles for 'best AI writing tools' 
and create a comprehensive outline that covers everything 
they cover plus unique angles they miss."
```

**Meta descriptions:**
```
"Write 5 meta description options for an article titled 
'Best AI Tools for Students in 2026.' Each should be 
under 155 characters, include the keyword, and have 
a compelling call to action."
```

**Schema markup:**
```
"Generate FAQ schema markup (JSON-LD) for these 5 questions 
and answers about AI writing tools."
```

## SEO Strategy with AI

### Step 1: Keyword Research
Use Semrush or Ahrefs to find keywords with:
- Decent search volume (100+ monthly searches)
- Low to medium difficulty (under 40 for new sites)
- Clear search intent
- Commercial potential

### Step 2: Content Planning
Create a content calendar targeting:
- 70% informational content (guides, how-tos, comparisons)
- 20% commercial content (reviews, best-of lists)
- 10% transactional content (deals, coupons, tools)

### Step 3: Content Creation
Use AI tools to:
- Generate outlines (Frase or ChatGPT)
- Write first drafts (Jasper or ChatGPT)
- Optimize for SEO (Surfer or NeuronWriter)
- Add unique insights and personal experience

### Step 4: On-Page Optimization
- Optimize title tags and meta descriptions
- Use proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Add internal links to related content
- Optimize images (alt text, compression)
- Add schema markup

### Step 5: Monitor & Improve
- Track rankings (Semrush or Ahrefs)
- Update content regularly
- Build backlinks
- Fix technical issues (site speed, mobile, crawl errors)
