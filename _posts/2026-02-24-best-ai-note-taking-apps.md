## Meeting Transcription

### Otter.ai - Best Meeting Transcription

Otter.ai records and transcribes meetings in real-time with impressive accuracy. It joins your Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet calls automatically.

**Features:**
- Real-time transcription during meetings
- AI-generated meeting summaries
- Action item extraction
- Speaker identification
- Searchable transcript archive
- Share highlights with team
- Integrates with Zoom, Teams, Google Meet

**How it works:**
1. Connect your calendar
2. Otter automatically joins your meetings
3. Real-time transcription appears during the call
4. After the meeting: summary, action items, and full transcript
5. Search across all past meetings

**Accuracy:** 85-95% depending on audio quality and accents.

**Price:** Free (300 min/month) / $17/month Pro / $25/month Business

### Fireflies.ai - Best for Teams

Fireflies.ai is similar to Otter but with stronger team collaboration and CRM integration features.

**Features:**
- Auto-join and transcribe meetings
- AI summaries with topics, action items, and questions
- Conversation intelligence (talk time, sentiment)
- CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Slack and Notion integration
- Custom vocabulary for industry terms
- API access

**Best for:** Sales teams, customer success, anyone who needs meeting data in their CRM.

**Price:** Free (limited) / $10/month Pro / $19/month Business

### tl;dv - Best for Video Meetings

tl;dv records video meetings and lets you create clips of key moments. Perfect for sharing meeting highlights.

**Features:**
- Record and transcribe Zoom/Teams/Meet
- AI timestamps key moments
- Create shareable video clips
- AI meeting notes and summaries
- Tag teammates in specific moments
- Multi-language transcription

**Best for:** Product teams, user research, anyone who needs to share meeting moments.

**Price:** Free (unlimited recordings) / $20/month Pro

## Note Organization

### Notion AI - Best All-in-One

Notion AI turns your notes into an intelligent workspace. It summarizes, organizes, and helps you find information across all your notes.

**AI note-taking features:**
- Summarize long meeting notes
- Generate action items from notes
- Q&A across your entire workspace ("What did we decide about the pricing?")
- Auto-fill database properties
- Translate notes
- Fix grammar and improve writing

**Why it's #1 for notes:**
- Notes, tasks, databases, and docs in one place
- Powerful organization (pages, databases, views)
- Team collaboration built in
- Templates for every use case
- API and integrations

**Price:** Free (limited AI) / $10/month Plus

### Mem - Best AI-Organized Notes

Mem takes a different approach: just write notes, and AI organizes them for you. No folders, no tags — AI handles the structure.

**How it works:**
- Write notes freely (no organization needed)
- AI automatically links related notes
- Search with natural language ("What was that idea about marketing?")
- AI surfaces relevant notes when you need them
- Smart collections group related content

**Why it's unique:** Most note apps require you to organize. Mem's philosophy is "just capture, AI will organize." It works surprisingly well.

**Price:** Free (limited) / $15/month Pro

### Reflect - Best for Networked Thinking

Reflect combines note-taking with AI to create a "second brain" that helps you think better.

**Features:**
- Backlinks and graph view (like Obsidian)
- AI assistant that knows all your notes
- Daily notes with AI summaries
- Meeting transcription built in
- End-to-end encryption
- Clean, fast interface

**Best for:** Knowledge workers who want to connect ideas across notes.

**Price:** $10/month

### Obsidian + AI Plugins - Best Local-First

Obsidian stores notes as local Markdown files. With AI plugins, it becomes a powerful AI note-taking system while keeping your data private.

**AI plugins:**
- **Smart Connections** — AI finds related notes
- **Copilot** — ChatGPT-like assistant in Obsidian
- **Text Generator** — AI writing assistance
- **Auto Classifier** — auto-tag and organize notes

**Why choose Obsidian:**
- Your notes are local files (you own them forever)
- Works offline
- Massive plugin ecosystem
- Graph view for connected thinking
- Free for personal use

**Price:** Free (personal) / $10/month for Sync

## Tips for AI Note-Taking

1. **Record everything** — storage is cheap, missed information is expensive
2. **Review AI summaries** — they're good but not perfect
3. **Add your own insights** — AI captures what was said, you capture what it means
4. **Use consistent formatting** — helps AI understand and organize better
5. **Search before creating** — AI search can find notes you forgot you had
6. **Weekly review** — spend 15 minutes reviewing and connecting notes

## Bottom Line

AI note-taking tools eliminate the anxiety of missing important information. Whether it's a meeting transcript, a brainstorm session, or daily notes, AI ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

Start with Otter.ai (free) for meeting transcription and Notion AI for note organization. Together, they cover 90% of what most people need.
