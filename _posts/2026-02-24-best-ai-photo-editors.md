## 1. Adobe Firefly - Best Professional AI Photo Editor

Adobe integrated Firefly AI directly into Photoshop, making it the most powerful AI photo editing solution available. Generative Fill and Generative Expand are game-changers.

**Key AI features:**
- **Generative Fill** — select an area and describe what you want to add or change
- **Generative Expand** — extend images beyond their original borders
- **AI background replacement** — swap backgrounds with a text prompt
- **Content-Aware Remove** — remove objects seamlessly
- **Neural Filters** — change expressions, age, lighting, and more
- **Sky replacement** — automatic sky detection and replacement

**Why it's #1:** Nothing else combines this level of AI capability with professional-grade editing tools. If you're serious about photo editing, this is the standard.

**Price:** $10/month (Photography plan with Photoshop + Lightroom)

## 2. Luminar Neo - Best One-Click AI Enhancements

Luminar Neo is designed for photographers who want powerful AI enhancements without the complexity of Photoshop. One click can transform a photo.

**Standout features:**
- **AI Enhance** — automatically adjusts exposure, contrast, and color
- **AI Sky Replacement** — realistic sky swaps with reflection matching
- **AI Skin Retouching** — natural-looking portrait enhancement
- **AI Structure** — enhance details without artifacts
- **AI Background Removal** — clean cutouts
- **GenErase** — remove objects with AI fill

**Best for:** Photographers who want quick, professional results without manual editing.

**Price:** $79/year or $149 lifetime

## 3. Remove.bg - Best Background Removal

Remove.bg does one thing and does it perfectly: remove backgrounds from photos in about 5 seconds.

**How it works:**
1. Upload your photo
2. AI instantly removes the background
3. Download with transparent background
4. Or choose a new background

**Why it's the best at this:**
- Works on complex subjects (hair, fur, transparent objects)
- API available for batch processing
- Integrations with Photoshop, Canva, and more
- Free for low-resolution downloads

**Price:** Free (low-res) / $9/month for HD downloads
