## How to Build an Effective AI Chatbot

1. **Define the scope** — what questions should it answer? What should it hand off to humans?
2. **Prepare your knowledge base** — gather FAQs, documentation, and common queries
3. **Set the tone** — match your brand voice (professional, casual, friendly)
4. **Test extensively** — try to break it before your customers do
5. **Monitor and improve** — review conversations weekly, update knowledge base
6. **Always offer human escalation** — never trap users in a bot loop
