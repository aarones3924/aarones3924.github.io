## On-Page Standards (Every Post)

- One clear primary keyword in title + first paragraph
- TOFU/MOFU/BOFU intent match before writing

- Actionable conclusion + next-step recommendation

- Publish: 4-6 posts/week
- Refresh: 2 older posts/week
- Internal linking sprint: 1 session/week
- Search Console review: 2 times/week
